Taking a job with Era Food Stores was one of the best decisions I have made. This company is full of opportunities and they are always open for new ideas, and everyone is treated like family.

Experienced Cook needed for busy production kitchen preparing ready meals, sandwiches and range of cafe food items for breakfast and lunch. You will be required to manage your own station and be responsible for set up, production and clean down of kitchen and be familiar with health and safety procedures.


Qualifications


• HACCP: 1 year (Required)

• Chef: 2 years (Preferred)


Job duties


• Prepare all food items as directed in a sanitary and timely manner

• Follow recipes and presentation specifications

• Operate standard kitchen equipment safely and efficiently

• Maintain a clean working station whilst adhering to health & safety standards

• Assist with the cleaning and organisation of kitchen and equipment

• Restock items as needed throughout the shift
