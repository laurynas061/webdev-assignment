I have joined Era Food Stores nearly 10 years ago, and I feel like I want to spend another 20 years here. My hard work has always been acknowledged. The company has very friendly environment with a lot of support to ensure that each worker achieves its goals.

You will help the Era Food Stores to stay number #1 in the industry, by ensuring the business continuity and you will help each shop to achieve its sales goals.


Requirements


• 3 years experience as a Brand Management or Marketing role.

• Degree Qualification in a related field.

• Experience in developing Brand strategies for sports and/or sport nutrition goods, designed for driving awareness, engagement and sales.

• Experience in Brand Activation, particularly online/social media content, event management and ambassador programmes.

• Competitor analysis and innovation experience.


Job duties


• Create the Brand Strategy to drive awareness, engagement and ultimately sales.

• Break the strategy into a time driven plan with expected results and milestones.

• Incorporate such things as Social Media content & Management, Ambassador programmes, Event attendance or management and eCommerce.

• Lead Innovations in brand activation and marketing.

• Monitor performance of the brand strategy, through expected volumes of sales.
